Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

The Short Version

Remember Who is built on a simple principle: your data stays on your device. We don't collect your contacts, notes, conversations, or location data. We don't have ads. We don't sell data. We can't read your information even if someone asked us to.

Who We Are

Remember Who is developed by Lost Pines Creative LLC, a single-member LLC based in Bastrop, Texas. You can reach us at support@rememberwho.app.

What Data We Collect

Almost nothing. Here's the full list:

  • Anonymous crash reports — via Sentry. These contain no personal data, only technical information about app crashes (device model, OS version, stack traces). No names, contacts, or notes are included.
  • App Store analytics — Apple and Google provide us aggregate download and usage statistics. We do not add any additional tracking.

That's it. We do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Your contacts or contact details
  • Your notes, interactions, or conversation transcriptions
  • Your location data
  • Your name, email, or phone number (unless you contact us directly)
  • Any usage patterns, behavior tracking, or analytics beyond crash reports

On-Device Processing

Remember Who uses on-device AI (Whisper) for voice transcription. Audio is processed locally on your phone and is never sent to any server. Transcription results are stored only in your local encrypted database.

Encryption

All sensitive data stored on your device is encrypted using AES-256 field-level encryption. Encryption keys are stored in your device's secure enclave (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore). We do not have access to your encryption keys.

Optional Cloud Backup

If you choose to enable cloud backup, your data is end-to-end encrypted before leaving your device. The encryption key is derived from your credentials and is never transmitted to our servers. This means:

  • We cannot read your backed-up data
  • We cannot decrypt it for law enforcement or anyone else
  • If you lose your encryption credentials, we cannot recover your data

Contact Sync

Remember Who can optionally sync with your phone's native contacts. This sync happens entirely on your device — contact data is read locally and never transmitted externally.

Third-Party Services

If you choose to connect Google or Microsoft calendar sync, those services have their own privacy policies. Remember Who only accesses calendar data you explicitly authorize, and this data is stored locally on your device.

Children's Privacy

Remember Who is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we change this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify users through the app.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at support@rememberwho.app.
